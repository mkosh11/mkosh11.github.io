Kintsugi 3D

User Experience research, design, and development
Research sponsored by the UW-Stout Office of Research and Sponsored Programs
May 2025-Present

Project Info Site

Viewer GitHub Repo

Kintsugi 3D is a software solution to enhance photogrammetry efforts at cultural heritage institutions with 2 applications: Builder and Viewer. My role in the project is contributing to the artifact configuration settings, which targets collection curators looking to configure properties for models to give public users the best experience when viewing models in the program. I am completing full-stack design and development of the user experience, including talking with stakeholders to learn goals and pain points, wireframing and ideating, designing and iterating on interface solutions, and finally implementing them using the Godot application.
